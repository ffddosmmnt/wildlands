## 1. WORLD STRUCTURE: THE ISLAND SYSTEM

### Core Concept: Dynamic Island Types

Durango's world isn't one continuous map. It's a **system of islands**, each with different progression rules, stability, and resources.

**Island Type Spectrum:**

```
TAMED ISLANDS (Safe, Stable)
    ↓
CIVILIZED ISLANDS (Moderate Risk, Paid Maintenance)
    ↓
UNSTABLE ISLANDS (High Risk, Disappearing)
    ↓
SAVAGE ISLANDS (Lawless, PvP-Enabled)
```

---

## 2. TAMED ISLANDS (Starter Zones)

**Purpose:** Safe starting zones where new survivors learn basics.

**Characteristics:**
- Permanent terrain (never disappears).
- Stable, renewable resources.
- Low-tier creatures (beginner-friendly).
- Safe for domain building (protected).
- Perfect for new players (2-20 hour experience).

**Biome Examples:**

### Coastal Valley
**Environment:**
- Beach access, gentle slopes, grassland interior.
- Temperate climate (no extreme weather).
- Water sources abundant (rivers, lakes).
- Visibility: Excellent (open sight lines).

**Flora:**
- Berry bushes (infinite, respawn 30 min).
- Oak trees (wood, respawn 1 hour).
- Edible plants (basic food).

**Fauna:**
- Deer herds (gather hide, bone, meat).
- Boar packs (gather hide, meat).
- Small predators (tier 1 combat).
- No apex creatures (safe zone).

**Resources:**
- Wood: Unlimited.
- Stone: Common ground loot.
- Hide/Meat: Herbivore drops.
- Water: Clean and safe.

**Progression Gate:**
- Entry: None (starting island).
- Exit: Gather 50+ hide, craft tier 2 gear, advance to Civilized Island.

---

### Grassland Plains
**Environment:**
- Flat, open terrain.
- Golden grass, scattered trees.
- Seasonal water (streams, seasonal flooding).
- Visibility: Good (slight hills block distance).

**Flora:**
- Wheat fields (cultivatable, Farmer specialty).
- Wildflowers (decoration, low value).
- Thorny bushes (defensive plants).

**Fauna:**
- Large herbivore herds (massive hunts).
- Pack hunters (coordinated, moderate threat).
- Ground nesting birds (egg gathering).

**Resources:**
- Abundant grain (Farmer's domain).
- Hide/Meat (consistent drops).
- Stones (scattered formations).

**Progression Gate:**
- Entry: Tier 1 (basic gear).
- Exit: Farming 20+ crops, join Farmer occupation.

---

## 3. CIVILIZED ISLANDS (Mid-Game Zones)

**Purpose:** Established territories where players can build clans, pay taxes, engage in light PvP.

**Characteristics:**
- Stable terrain (long-term settlements).
- Renewable but scarcer resources (respawn 2-4 hours).
- Mix of tier 1-2 creatures (moderate challenge).
- Requires T-Stone taxation to maintain domain.
- Clan territories (competitive but limited conflict).

**Biome Examples:**

### Mountain Foothills
**Environment:**
- Elevated terrain, caves, rocky outcrops.
- Temperate-cold climate (cold weather possible).
- Multiple altitudinal zones.
- Visibility: Variable (valleys block sight, peaks open).

**Flora:**
- Alpine trees (stronger wood).
- Mountain herbs (medicinal).
- Rare crystal formations (visual landmarks).

**Fauna:**
- Bears (territorial, aggressive).
- Mountain predators (intelligent).
- Eagles (non-hostile, visual).
- Rare exotic creatures.

**Resources:**
- Copper ore deposits (tier 2 material).
- Stone (high quality).
- Alpine herbs (alchemy ingredients).
- Rare drops (skill challenges).

**Progression Gate:**
- Entry: Tier 2 gear mandatory.
- Exit: Defeat apex creatures, gather rare materials.

---

### Dense Forest
**Environment:**
- Thick vegetation, limited visibility.
- Tropical-temperate mix.
- Water abundance (rivers, waterfalls).
- Visibility: Poor (15-20 meter sight lines).

**Flora:**
- Ancient trees (valuable wood).
- Rare herbs (deep forest exclusive).
- Vines (rope material).
- Mushroom fields (poison/edible mix).

**Fauna:**
- Jaguars/big cats (apex, intelligent).
- Snakes (non-hostile but scary).
- Monkeys (gathering helpers, non-hostile).
- Pack hunters (coordinated).

**Resources:**
- Hardwood (tier 2-3 material).
- Rare herbs (alchemy focus).
- Venom sacs (poison materials).
- Hidden treasures (secret locations).

**Progression Gate:**
- Entry: Tier 2 gear + stamina management.
- Exit: Hunt apex predators, gather rare herbs.

## 4. UNSTABLE ISLANDS (End-Game Zones)

**Purpose:** High-risk, high-reward expedition zones. Appear and disappear dynamically.

**Characteristics:**
- Temporary terrain (appears 6-12 hours, disappears 6-12 hours).
- Highest-tier resources (rare, limited nodes).
- Tier 3 creatures (apex predators, bosses).
- No permanent bases (prevents camping).
- Multiple expeditions needed per cycle.
- Extreme rewards (legendary materials, unique drops).

**Biome Examples:**

### Volcanic Peak
**Environment:**
- Volcanic landscape, lava flows, geysmal activity.
- Extreme heat (damage-over-time without prep).
- Rocky, dangerous terrain (fall hazards).
- Limited visibility (ash, lava glow).

**Flora:**
- Heat-resistant plants (rare materials).
- Sulfur deposits (alchemy ingredients).
- Flame flowers (magical components).

**Fauna:**
- Fire-breathing creatures (heat-based attacks).
- Lava serpents (aquatic magma dwellers).
- Rare phoenix variants (legendary).

**Resources:**
- Mithril ore (ultra-rare).
- Sulfur (alchemy essential).
- Flame gems (magical).
- Phoenix feathers (legendary drop).

**Hazards:**
- Lava damage (constant threat).
- Toxic air (poison over time).
- Falling rocks (arena hazards).
- Environmental collapse (island disappears).

**Strategy:**
- Heat resistance gear mandatory.
- Quick expeditions (4-6 hour limit).
- High-reward targets (worth the risk).

---

### Frozen Wastes
**Environment:**
- Arctic tundra, ice fields, blizzards.
- Extreme cold (damage-over-time without prep).
- Visibility: Terrible (blizzard, white-out conditions).
- Landmarks obscured (navigation by compass only).

**Flora:**
- Cold-resistant plants (rare materials).
- Ice crystals (magical components).
- Frozen trees (rare lumber).

**Fauna:**
- Ice creatures (frost-based attacks).
- Polar predators (ambush hunters).
- Rare ice dragons (legendary).

**Resources:**
- Mithril ore (alternate source).
- Ice crystals (unique material).
- Frost gems (magical).
- Ice dragon scales (legendary).

**Hazards:**
- Cold damage (constant threat).
- Blizzard storms (visibility loss, disorientation).
- Avalanches (environmental collapse).

**Strategy:**
- Cold resistance gear mandatory.
- Quick expeditions (same 4-6 hour limit).
- Compass navigation (GPS unreliable).

---

### Primordial Jungle
**Environment:**
- Rainforest, dense vegetation, misty valleys.
- High humidity, constant rainfall.
- Thick canopy (no sky visibility).
- Wildlife density: Extreme.

**Flora:**
- Legendary plants (apex herbalism).
- Exotic flowers (unique alchemy).
- Ancient vines (rope, binding).

**Fauna:**
- All apex predators appear here.
- Rare dinosaur species (tier 4).
- Legendary creatures (unique bosses).

**Resources:**
- Rare herbs (ultimate alchemy).
- Exotic materials (no other source).
- Boss drops (legendary unique items).

**Hazards:**
- Disease (jungle fever, debuff).
- Predators (constant ambush risk).
- Environmental complexity (navigation hard).
- Swamp hazards (drowning risk).

**Strategy:**
- Disease resistance (optional potions).
- Full exploration team (not solo).
- Multiple expeditions (needs many runs).

## 5. SAVAGE ISLANDS (PvP-Enabled)

**Purpose:** Lawless territories where players can engage in clan wars, territory control, and unrestricted PvP.

**Characteristics:**
- High-risk, high-reward.
- No NPC protection (players can steal, raid).
- Extreme resources (worth the danger).
- Permanent fixture (doesn't disappear).
- Clan warfare encouraged (territory control quests).
- Optional engagement (not mandatory for V1).

**Example: Warlord Plateau**
- Contested territory between clans.
- Control provides T-Stone discounts, resource bonuses.
- Regular warfare events (organized battle times).
- Penalty for death (lose carried items permanently).

## 6. ENVIRONMENTAL MECHANICS

### Time of Day Cycle

**Daytime (12 hours in-game):**
- Visibility: 100%.
- Creature behavior: Normal, predictable.
- Temperature: Comfortable.
- Safety: Optimal for exploration.

**Nighttime (12 hours in-game):**
- Visibility: 20% (darkness, lamps help).
- Creature behavior: Nocturnal creatures active, diurnal resting.
- Temperature: Cold (drain stamina if unprepared).
- Safety: Dangerous (ambush risk +300%).

**Transitions (1 hour dusk/dawn):**
- Visibility: 60% (twilight).
- Creature behavior: Mixed (both active).
- Planning phase (prepare for night shift).

**Strategic Implications:**
- Plan hunts around daylight.
- Nocturnal hunting = high reward, high risk.
- Base camp safe at night (creature attacks reduced).
- Time pressure (return before dark).

### Weather System

**Weather Effects:**

| Type | Duration | Effect | Hazard |
|------|----------|--------|--------|
| Clear | 50% | Nothing | N/A |
| Rain | 25% | Slippery (-20% move speed) | Minor |
| Fog | 15% | Visibility -50% | Medium |
| Storm | 10% | Wind, heavy rain, lightning | High |

**Seasonal Variations (Post-V1):**
- Spring: Abundant spawning (easy hunting).
- Summer: Dry, fire hazards.
- Fall: Migration (creatures relocate).
- Winter: Extreme cold, scarcity.

### Environmental Hazards

**Damage Sources:**
- Lava/extreme heat (5 HP/sec without protection).
- Freezing cold (3 HP/sec without protection).
- Poison gas (2 HP/sec without antidote).
- Drowning (instant if go too deep).
- Fall damage (10 HP per 2-meter fall).

**Avoidance:**
- Gear provides resistance (heat-resistant armor, etc.).
- Potions provide immunity (temporary).
- Positioning avoids hazards (stay away from lava).

## 7. DOMAIN SYSTEM (Domain Placement)

### Tamed Island Domains

**Placement Rules:**
- Claim plot on Tamed Island (limited slots per island).
- No limit on building if space available.
- Protected from creature attacks (fortifications not mandatory).
- Safe place for crafting, storage, rest.

**Domain Progression:**

**Tier 1 (Hours 0-10):**
- Single dwelling (30 storage).
- 1-2 workstations.
- Basic fence (decoration).

**Tier 2 (Hours 10-30):**
- Multiple dwellings (60 storage).
- 4-6 workstations.
- Defensive walls (creature protection).

**Tier 3 (Hours 30+):**
- Settlement compound (150+ storage).
- 8-12 workstations.
- Towers, fortifications (clan defense).

### Civilized Island Domains

**Maintenance Cost:**
- 100-500 T-Stones per day (scales with plot size).
- Paid from treasury (clan resource pool).
- Unpaid → domain becomes unclaimed (lost to another clan).
- Economic pressure (encourages clan cooperation).

## 8. EXPLORATION & DISCOVERY

### Discovery Mechanics

**Resource Indicators:**
- Ore deposits glow faintly (ore-colored glow).
- Rare herbs shimmer (magical sparkle).
- Hidden caves marked (subtle visual cue).
- Water sources sparkle (safe vs. dangerous).

**Secret Locations:**
- Hidden caves (rare material nodes).
- Ancient ruins (lore, secret quests).
- Sacred sites (faction-specific content).
- Treasure caches (unique items, one-time).

### Map Revelation

**Fog of War:**
- Unexplored areas are gray (unknown).
- Explored areas show terrain (permanent memory).
- POIs reveal nearby terrain (discovery opens map).
- High vantage points reveal large areas (climbing reward).
